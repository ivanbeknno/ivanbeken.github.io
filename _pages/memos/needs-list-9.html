--- layout: single title: "Basic needs shared by all" description: "A list of universal needs organized into 9 categories" published: true permalink: /needs/ --- Basic needs shared by all.
Divided into 9 categories:

1. Subsistence and Survival

  • Survival
  • Food
  • Water
  • Sleep
  • Cleanliness
  • Health (Safe and healthy environment)
  • Shelter (Habitable environment)
  • Rest
  • Movement, Exercise
  • Sexual relief

2. Safety and Protection

  • Safety
  • Trust
  • Order (things in their proper place)
  • Confidence in the future
  • Protection
  • Support
  • Comfort
  • Own space

3. Love and Affection

The need can be to receive,
as well as expressions of care, intimacy, love, etc.
  • Closeness
  • Care
  • Attention (to one's worries and problems)
  • Love
  • Friendship
  • Warmth
  • Support
  • Appreciation
  • Integrity
  • Respect
  • Comfort
  • Acceptance
  • Forgiveness (acceptance and understanding)

4. Empathy and Understanding

  • Warm attitudes
  • Being heard
  • Sharing feelings
  • Expressing feelings
  • Grieving, mourning, living with and accepting loss
  • Celebration of important joyful events

5. Self-expression and Creativity

  • Self-expression
  • Creativity
  • Inspiration
  • Contact with yourself
  • Understanding our own desires
  • Peace
  • Harmony
  • Beauty

6. Recreation, Idleness, and Play

  • Play
  • Playfulness
  • Fun
  • Rest
  • Rest and serenity
  • Relaxation

7. Community Involvement and Sense of belonging

  • Belonging
  • Involvement
  • Mutual understanding
  • Warmth in the community
  • Interaction
  • Acceptance by the team/community
  • Respect
  • Acceptance
  • Trust
  • Appreciation

8. Freedom and Independence

  • Self-Esteem
  • Self-Acceptance
  • Self-confidence
  • The ability to choose your own dreams, goals, and values
  • Ability to choose your own plan to achieve them
  • Freedom to make decisions

9. Contribution, Significance, and Passion

  • The need to see meaning in what you do
  • Development
  • Effectiveness
  • Meaningfulness
